About Coreminds Academy, Hojai

Coreminds Academy is a modern tuition centre in Hojai built for students of Classes 6 to 10 who want disciplined learning, stronger fundamentals, and confident exam performance in both English and Assamese medium.

Our Mission

We help students grow beyond marks by building clarity, consistency, and confidence. Every class follows a structured plan with concept teaching, practice sheets, doubts, and performance review.

Parents choose Coreminds Academy because we maintain a focused class culture, clear communication, and measurable progress through regular tests and feedback.

What Makes Us Different

Concept + Application

Students learn how to think, not just memorize.

Small Batch Attention

Each student gets close monitoring and doubt support.

Strong Academic Routine

Weekly targets and revision cycles keep students on track.
